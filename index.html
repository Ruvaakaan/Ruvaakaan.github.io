About

Musashi Miyamoto (みやもとむさし, Miyamoto Musashi) is one of the main protagonists in Vagabond, and a historical figure considered one of the greatest swordsmen in Japanese history. As a young man he embarks on a journey to become the strongest samurai after being exiled from his village. He was born under the name Takezou Shinmen (しんめんたけぞう, Shinmen Takezo), but later changed it to Musashi Miyamoto.
